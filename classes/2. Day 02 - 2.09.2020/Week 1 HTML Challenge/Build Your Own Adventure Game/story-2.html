Story 2: Assignment

Badger Squad

Assigned to Badger Squad with fellow Marines Sergeant Cormack and Private Jackson, the squad inserts by drop pod into Seoul but are thrown off course due to the North Korean's anti-air defenses. Badger Squad manages to regroup with friendly forces and are assigned to assist a demolitions team with the overall objective of destroying an enemy aerial launcher.

Mission targets:

  1. Badger squad to regroup with friendly forces
  2. Assist a demolition team with the overall objective
